Cookie Policy

How Spainza uses cookies and similar technologies. Effective June 6, 2026.

1General provisions

This Cookie Policy describes the use of cookies and similar technologies on spainza.com and the client portal (the “Service”) operated by Spainza.

By continuing to use the Service, you agree to strictly necessary cookies as described here and in our Privacy Policy. Disabling necessary cookies may limit functionality, including portal sign-in.

2What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your device. They help recognize sessions, remember settings, and maintain security. localStorage and sessionStorage in the browser may serve a similar role.

3Technologies we use

Strictly necessary (technical):

  • access_token (HTTP cookie) — authentication in the client portal and access to protected APIs;
  • token, currentUserProfile (localStorage) — session maintenance and profile display in the UI;
  • spainza.language, userLocale (localStorage) — saved interface language preference.

Functional: short-lived sessionStorage data (e.g., manager invite token) to complete specific sign-in flows.

Analytics and marketing: as of the publication date, Spainza does not use third-party advertising or analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.) on the Service. If this changes, this Policy will be updated.

4Retention

Session cookies are removed when you sign out or when the token expires. localStorage data remains until you clear it in browser settings or until the app clears relevant keys on sign-out.

5Managing cookies

You may restrict or delete cookies and localStorage data in your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent portal sign-in and use of protected features.

Instructions are usually available under Privacy or Cookies in your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, etc.).

6Third-party resources

The Service may load fonts and other assets from CDNs (e.g., Google Fonts). Such providers may process technical data (IP address, user agent) under their own policies. Spainza does not control third-party cookies on their domains.

You independently assess risks when following external links or using third-party services.

7Policy updates

We may update this Policy. The current version is always available on this page.
